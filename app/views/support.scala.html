Frequently Asked Questions

How do I let my student know their schedule?

Before each student’s rotation, you will need to click on the “Email schedule” button. This can be found under the Students tab.

Where do my students report for clinic? Are there any lectures?

Start times by clinic site:

Clinic Site Floor Start Time
The Emory Clinic (TEC)
1525 Clifton Road		 3rd Floor 8:20am* & 1:00pm
*Except Thursday mornings start at 10am
Grady Clinic 1st Floor (A/B Tower) 8:15am & 1:15pm
VA Clinic 1st Floor (medicine subspecialties clinic area) 8:30am* & 1:30pm
*Except Tuesday mornings start at 9:30am

We also welcome rotators to attend Emory Dermatology Grand Rounds, which are held on the 1st, 3rd, and 5th Thursday of the month. It is in the Brown Auditorium at Emory Clinic Building A (1365 Clifton Road) from 7:45am to 10am.

What if there is not an open or available clinic during a time that I want to register a student?

Unfortunately, due to high volume and pre-existing agreements, we are not able to accommodate all requests. Please check back in the future to see if any pots become available.

What if I registered a student for a rotation, but he/she can no longer attend?

You can do this through the same calendar that you used to initially register the student. Please see “New user instructions” document for details. If a student has to cancel a rotation, please do so on the calendar as soon as possible so that that spot may be available to other students who are interested.

I received an email that a clinic that I registered a student for has been canceled. What does this mean?

Unfortunately, the clinic you registered for is no longer in session that day. Please go back to the main calendar you used to register the student to see if there are any alternative options available

I still have questions. Is there someone I can call?

Please call our dermatology rotation coordinator at 404-727-3669 with any questions. Please leave a detailed message with how we can reach you.
