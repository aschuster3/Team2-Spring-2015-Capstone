Frequently Asked Questions

How do I let my student know their schedule?

Before each student’s rotation, you will need to click on the “Email schedule” button. This can be found under the Students tab.

Where do my students report for clinic? Are there any lectures?

Start times by clinic site:

Clinic Site Floor Start Time
The Emory Clinic (TEC)
1525 Clifton Road		 3rd Floor 8:20am* & 1:00pm
*Except Thursday mornings start at 10am
Grady Clinic 1st Floor (A/B Tower) 8:15am & 1:15pm
VA Clinic 1st Floor (medicine subspecialties clinic area) 8:30am* & 1:30pm
*Except Tuesday mornings start at 9:30am

We also welcome rotators to attend Emory Dermatology Grand Rounds, which are held on the 1st, 3rd, and 5th Thursday of the month. It is in the Brown Auditorium at Emory Clinic Building A (1365 Clifton Road) from 7:45am to 10am.

What if there is not an open or available clinic during a time that I want to register a student?

Unfortunately, due to high volume and pre-existing agreements, we are not able to accommodate all requests. Please check back in the future to see if any pots become available.

What if I registered a student for a rotation, but he/she can no longer attend?

You can do this through the same calendar that you used to initially register the student. Please see “New user instructions” document for details. If a student has to cancel a rotation, please do so on the calendar as soon as possible so that that spot may be available to other students who are interested.

I received an email that a clinic that I registered a student for has been canceled. What does this mean?

Unfortunately, the clinic you registered for is no longer in session that day. Please go back to the main calendar you used to register the student to see if there are any alternative options available

I still have questions. Is there someone I can call?

Please call our dermatology rotation coordinator at 404-727-3669 with any questions. Please leave a detailed message with how we can reach you.

The Login Screen

Currently registered users

  • If you know your credentials, enter your email and password into the appropriate boxes and click the “Login” button

  • If you have forgotten your password, click the “Forgot your password?” link towards the bottom of the page

    1. On the following page, enter your e-mail address into the box provided and click the “Submit” button

    2. An email will be sent to your email address containing a link

    3. Click this link and you will be directed to a page where you will be prompted to reset your password

    4. Once you’ve reset your password and clicked “Submit”, you will be logged in to the system

    • Not currently registered users

  • Request Access through the link, “Don’t have an account? Sign up”, at the bottom of the page

    1. On the following page, enter your first name, last name and your e-mail address in the appropriate boxes and select your department from the drop down list. Then, click the “Register” button to submit your information as a request for credentials to be able to login to and use the application.

    2. Once you submit your request, an administrator will have to approve your credentials

    3. If and when you are approved by the administrator, you will receive an e-mail with a link to a screen where you can create your password

    4. Once you enter your password into the correct field and confirm it, click the “Submit” button and you will automatically be logged into the system

    • External Coordinator Functions

    Log out

  • To logout of the application, click the red “Log Out” button located on the far right of the navigation bar

    • View your current students

  • Navigate to the Students page by clicking the “Students” Link in the navigation bar

  • Under the title, “Your Students”, each of your student’s names should appear in their own individual box

    • View more information about a particular student

  • Click on the student’s name(the text should change color when your mouse is hovering over the text) and the student’s individual box should expand.

  • Once their individual box is expanded, you should be able to see the student’s name, e-mail address, the type of student they are, and a list of all the events they are currently signed up for.

  • To collapse this box, click the student’s name at the top again.

    • Edit a particular student’s information

  • Click on the student’s name(the text should change color when your mouse is hovering over the text) and the student’s individual box should expand.

  • Once their individual box is expanded, you should see a yellow “Edit” button in the bottom left corner of the expanded box. Click on this button.

  • Once you click on this button, input fields should appear for each aspect of the student’s personal information.

  • Change whatever information you wish to in these fields.

  • Click the blue “Save” button to save your changes when you are finished.

    • E-mail a particular student their schedule

  • Navigate to the Students page by clicking the “Students” Link in the navigation bar.

  • Click on a student’s name to expand their individual box.

  • Once the box has been expanded, you should see a blue button in the upper right corner of this box that says, “E-mail Schedule to Student”. Click this button.

  • A modal will pop up confirming that the email was sent. The email will contain their schedule and any necessary supplemental information.

    • E-mail all of your students their schedules

  • Navigate to the Students page by clicking the “Students” link in the navigation bar.

  • Towards the bottom left of the page, there is a blue button labelled “E-mail All Students”. Click this button.

  • A modal will appear asking you to confirm that you want to continue with this action. If you wish to cancel, click the blue "Cancel" button at the bottom right of the modal.

  • If you wish to continue, click the blue "Confirm" button in the bottom right of the modal and an email will be sent to all students containing their schedule and any necessary supplemental information.

    • Add a new student

  • Navigate to the Students page by clicking the “Students” link in the navigation bar.

  • At the bottom left of the page, find the blue button, “Add Students”, and click it.

  • A form will open up underneath the button.

  • Enter the student’s name, e-mail into the appropriate fields and select their type from the dropdown menu.

  • Click the blue “Submit” button at the bottom left of the form to submit the student.

    • View the calendar of events

  • Navigate to the calendar containing all the events, click the “Schedule” link in the navigation bar.

  • The page you are directed to will display a calendar in a month-view format.

  • If a day contains any events, a blue circle will be present in the top left corner of the day and contain a number for how many events are on that day.

    • View events on a particular day

  • Navigate to the calendar containing all the events, click the “Schedule” link in the navigation bar.

  • Navigate to your desired month (or week depending on which view you are currently in) using the “Next” and “Previous” buttons located above the calendar.

  • Find the day you are looking for and and click anywhere within that day’s box.

    1. If a day does not have any events scheduled and therefore no blue dot with a number inside of it appears within that day, the day will not be clickable

  • A panel will expand below the day with a list of all that day’s events and their details such as the location of the event, the doctor associated with the event and whether it is an AM or PM event.

    1. If the dot next to an event is yellow, the event is available for scheduling.

    2. If the dot next to an event is gray, the event is not available for scheduling.

  • To collapse this panel, just click anywhere within that day’s box again.

    • Sign a student up for an event

  • Navigate to the calendar containing all the events by clicking the “Schedule” link in the navigation bar.

  • In the top right above the calendar, there is a dropdown menu that says “Select Student”. Select the student who you want to sign up for an event from this dropdown menu.

  • Find the event in the calendar that you want to sign this student up for.

  • If the event has a yellow dot next to it, the event is open and the student you selected is eligible to sign up for it. If the dot is gray, then either the event has already been taken or your student is not eligible to sign up for it.

  • To sign your student up for this event if the dot is yellow, click the plus sign that appears to the right of the event details.

  • Note: It is possible for a yellow event to actually be taken (Suppose two coordinators open the app on their own computers simultaneously. If the first coordinator signs their learner up for an event, the second coordinator will not see this change in real-time). In this case, you will see an error notification saying that the event has already been taken, and the event will now be grayed out.

  • If the session you choose to sign up for is part of a group of sessions, a pop up will appear to notify you.

  • Any sessions that are gray because your student is not eligible for it will open up to all types of students two weeks before the event occurs if it has not already been filled, so be sure to check back within that time frame if your student needs more sessions

    • Unregister a student from an event

  • Navigate to the calendar containing all the events by clicking the “Schedule” link in the navigation bar.

  • In the top right above the calendar, there is a dropdown menu that says “Select Student”. Select the student who you want to unregister for an event from this dropdown menu.

  • Find the event in the calendar that you want to unregister this student from.

  • Next to this event there should be an “X” icon. Click this icon and your student should be unregistered for that event.

  • You should see the “X” icon change back to a “+” icon after you have clicked it. This confirms that you were successful in unregistering your student from the event.
